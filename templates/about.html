The Story Behind MyJournal

MyJournal was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves a beautiful, private space to capture their thoughts and experiences. As a computer science student at JECRC University, I recognized the importance of mental wellness and self-reflection in our fast-paced digital world.

After participating in Smart India Hackathon 2023 with the Sukoon mental wellness project, I was inspired to create a dedicated platform for daily journaling. MyJournal combines modern web technologies with thoughtful design to make journaling accessible, enjoyable, and meaningful.

Whether you're documenting your daily experiences, tracking your personal growth, or simply need a place to organize your thoughts, MyJournal is here to support your journey of self-discovery.
